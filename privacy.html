Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

The short version

We only collect the details you choose to send us, we use them only to reply to you, and we don't sell them to anyone.

What we collect

When you use our contact or feedback form, we collect the information you enter — typically your name, your phone number or email, and your message. We don't ask for, or store, payment details on this website.

How we use it

We use your details to respond to your enquiry, confirm bookings or large orders, and follow up about feedback. We do not use your details for marketing unless you've asked us to.

How forms are handled

Our forms are delivered to us by a third-party form service (FormSubmit), which passes your message to our email inbox. Your message is handled under that provider's privacy practices in transit.

Keeping it safe

We keep enquiry details only as long as needed to deal with your request, and take reasonable steps to protect them.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and ask us to correct it. Contact us and we'll help.
