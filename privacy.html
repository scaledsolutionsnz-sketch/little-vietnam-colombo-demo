Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

Little Vietnam Colombo respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it, in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. (This is placeholder content for demonstration and should be reviewed before going live.)

1. What we collect

When you send a booking enquiry or submit feedback, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email, phone number and your message. We don't ask for more than we need.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your booking or enquiry and confirm your visit.
  • To read and act on feedback about your experience.
  • To contact you about your request when needed.

3. How forms are handled

Our website forms are delivered to the restaurant by a third-party form service (FormSubmit). Your submission is sent to our email address and is not published publicly. We don't use this site to sell or share your details with marketers.

4. Cookies & analytics

This site uses minimal cookies needed for it to work. Any analytics, if enabled, are used only to understand general website usage and never to identify you personally.

5. Keeping your information

We keep enquiry and feedback details only as long as needed to respond to you and to run the restaurant, then dispose of them securely.

6. Your rights

Under New Zealand law you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Get in touch and we'll help.

7. Contact

For any privacy question, email littlevietnam.nz@gmail.com or call 022 188 7519.
