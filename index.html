Our story

A little piece of Vietnam on Colombo Street

Little Vietnam Colombo brings the food we love — the bowls and baguettes you'd find on a street corner in Saigon — to the middle of Christchurch. The broths take hours, the herbs are cut fresh, and the welcome is the kind you'd get at a family table.

Whether you're after a quick bánh mì on your lunch break or settling in for a long, shared dinner, we cook it the same way: properly, generously, and with good flavour first.

  • Locally owned and run, right in central Christchurch
  • Broths and sauces made in-house, not from a packet
  • Dine in, takeaway, or call ahead — your call
