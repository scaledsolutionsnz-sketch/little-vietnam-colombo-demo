About us

A little corner of Vietnam in Ōtautahi

Little Vietnam Colombo is a small family kitchen on Colombo Street, cooking the food we grew up on for Christchurch locals, students and city workers. Everything is made to order — no heat lamps, no shortcuts.

Whether you're after a quick banh mi between meetings or settling in for a big bowl of pho on a cold Canterbury night, you'll get the real thing: fresh herbs, proper broths and a warm welcome.

  • Family owned and run, right here on Colombo Street
  • Made-to-order with fresh herbs and daily-prepped broths
  • Dine in, takeaway, and plenty of vegetarian & vegan options
